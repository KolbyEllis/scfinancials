About Noah

Noah grew up as a pastor's kid in Spokane. Attending Grand Canyon University, he got his degree in Finance and Economics. During the summers, he worked on Alaskan fishing boats to graduate debt-free. He met his wife Jenny at Taurenhof, a bible school in Austria. He loves the Pacific Northwest and recharges by playing pickleball, snowboarding, backpacking, and camping trips with his wife, Jenny. His two favorite trips were when he rode a motorbike the length of Vietnam and visited all of the Lord of the Rings movie locations in New Zealand.

He is a frequent featured expert on many popular financial news sources. He writes for the Spokane Journal of Business and is a featured expert on many websites such as Yahoo News, MarketWatch, U.S. News, Next Avenue, and GoBankingRates. Noah is passionate about financial literacy. He is writing a financial education book and has helped facilitate Financial Peace University at his church, True Hope. He is a FPA, CFP, NAPFA, and Spokane Financial Advisors Network member.
