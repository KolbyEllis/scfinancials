What is Financial Planning?

Financial planning is a 6-step process where our financial advisor looks at what you have, identifies goals, and gives actionable steps to achieve them. While identifying any missing financial pieces in your current plan. This is a snapshot. Regularly updating the plan to account for changes is important.

  • Discover

    Initial meeting with no charge and no commitment. We determine if we can assist you.

  • Establish

    Determining and establishing realistic financial goals such as retirment date, education targets for children, home purchase, etc.

  • Gather

    As accurate information is key to an accurate plan, gathering all necessary documents and data, such as expenses, pay stubs, assets, liabilities, etc.

  • Develop

    Creating a financial roadmanp, including key reccomendation and action steps on topics including cash flow management, tax planning, retirement planning, risk management, and estate planning.

  • Implement

    Implementing all mutually agreed upoin reccomendations and action steps to achieve financial goals

  • Monitor

    Life is unpredictable. We will update the plan to take into account any modification in assumptions or material life changes.
