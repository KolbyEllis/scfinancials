About Amy

Amy was born and raised in Spokane. She attended Whitworth University, where she obtained her degree in Mathematical Economics. After college, Amy started her career at Numerica Credit Union, where she worked in the investment department for five years.

She joined Stewardship Concepts in 2020 and is passionate about helping clients be good stewards of their money. Amy loves to stay active with her husband and two daughters. She enjoys reading, cooking, and cross-country skiing in her free time. Amy is a CFP and serves as treasurer for the Spokane Financial Planning Association.
